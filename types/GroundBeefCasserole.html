Ground Beef Casserole

Description

Old-fashioned hamburger casserole featuring ground beef, noodles, and a creamy cheese sauce. This simple recipe dates back to the 1940's!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Seasonings:
    • white sugar
    • salt
    • garlic salt
  • Pasta
  • Sour Cream and Cream Cheese
  • Onion
  • Chedder Cheese

    • Steps

    1. Cook and season the ground beef
    2. Cook egg noodles
    3. Mix sour cream and cream cheese
    4. assemble and bake
